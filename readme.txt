Meet Tidio Elements Integrator, a solution, which allows you to develop your site with tens of plugins including a Newsletter, Chat and many others!

== Description ==

Would you like to have your own plugin site such as Chat, Cookie Policy, or Feedback? It's simple! Just install the Tidio Elements Integrator extension, which will help you connect your site to Tidio Elements in a quick and easy way.

[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KK6ptPwbgLE]

= Why should you integrate your site with Tidio Elements: =
* Tens of functional plugins available at a single click, including Chat, Feedback, Newsletter and others
* Quick installation - all you need to do to integrate your site with our service is install one extension!
* Personalization - adapt the colors of our solutions to the graphic layout of your site in just a few minutes
* Free of charge - Yes! our services are completely free of charge
* An interface, which you will fall in love with - adding new functionalities in Tidio Elements is a matter of 2-3 clicks, you choose the extension, you define its position and it's ready! It could not get any simpler

All you need to do is open the "Tidio Elements" tab after installing the extension and log in, or set up a new account and it's done! Your site is now integrated with Tidio Elements!

== Installation ==
1. Go to WordPress Control Panel
1. Click "Plugins", then "Add New"
1. Type in "Tidio Elements Integrator" and click "Search Plugins"
1. Download and install the Plugin
1. Click the "Activate Plugin" link
1. Move to the "Tidio Elements" section in the menu
1. You log in or set up your accoun
1. You choose a project to edit
1. Ready! Your site is integrated with our service
